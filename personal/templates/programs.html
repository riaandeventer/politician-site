Political Programs


Education


Education is one of the most powerful tools that help society develop new professionals in various professions, from engineers to designers. However, some significant improvements are needed in this area. Carl Tredoux's education reform emphasizes local control of education, providing instructional materials for teachers, and improving curricula for college students.



Social


In order to improve the social aspect of life in our community, Carl Tredoux has instituted a new social responsibility policy for local governments, large corporations, and businesses that deal with our citizens. The new social program developed by Mr. Tredoux's team is based on several legislative improvements to reduce crime and poverty and increase employment opportunities and overall economic and financial standards.



Agriculture


As the years go by, our country becomes more and more vulnerable as global warming affects all countries in the world. Despite high agricultural profits, American agriculture suffers environmental and unemployment consequences. This is the main reason why Joseph Parker's political program can be useful for our country, especially with regard to agriculture. Mr. Parker wants to change that by providing more jobs for agricultural specialists and more resources for them.
