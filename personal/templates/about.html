About Carl Tredoux


Carl Tredoux is connected to the people of our province and the challenges they face every day. He is working to break down the old partisan barriers in our society that limit and slow down the development of our province.


Carl Tredoux was innovative, and persistent, put his party affiliation aside, and brought people together to accomplish things for our state. Mr. Tredoux has focused on creating new jobs, improving economic opportunities for all residents and guests of our state, addressing the economic bottleneck for middle-class families, and overall improvement. He sees his role as helping the people of our state by bringing improvements to the Senate level. This allows the attention of government economic and policy experts to be focused on the state's problems that begin in small towns.


Carl's Mission

Education

Education has always been one of Mr. Tredoux's greatest concerns, not only because it's often neglected by other politicians, but also because Carl Tredoux is a former school teacher.

Social

To improve the social aspect of life in our community, Carl Tredoux has instituted a new social responsibility policy for local governments, major corporations, and businesses that deal with our citizens.

Agriculture

Agriculture is often not a priority in modern politics, and therefore its improvement became one of Carl's main goals. Achieving more in this area means a lot for the economy of our state.
